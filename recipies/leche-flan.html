Leche-Flan

Description:

Leche Flan is a rich and creamy custard dessert, popular in the Philippines. Made with a smooth blend of egg yolks, sweetened condensed milk, and evaporated milk, this luscious treat is topped with a golden caramel sauce. Perfect for celebrations and special occasions, its silky texture and caramelized sweetness make it an irresistible delight.


Ingredients:


  • 1 cup sugar (for caramel)
  • 10 egg yolks
  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can (12 oz) evaporated milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Make the Caramel:
    • In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the sugar until it turns into a light brown caramel. Swirl the pan occasionally to ensure even melting.
    • Once the caramel is ready, pour it into a llanera (flan mold) or a round baking pan, tilting the mold to coat the bottom evenly. Set aside to cool and harden.
  2. Prepare the Custard: In a separate bowl, mix the graham cracker crumbs with the melted butter until well combined.
    • In a large bowl, whisk the egg yolks gently until well blended.
    • Add the sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth and well combined, avoiding creating too many bubbles.
  3. Strain the Mixture:For a smoother texture, strain the custard mixture through a fine sieve into another bowl to remove any lumps or bubbles.
  4. Pour into mold:Pour the strained custard mixture into the prepared mold with the caramel.
  5. Steam or Bake:
    • Steaming Method:Cover the mold with aluminum foil. Steam over medium heat for about 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
    • Baking Method:Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Place the mold in a larger baking dish filled with hot water (water bath). Cover the mold with aluminum foil and bake for about 45-50 minutes, or until the custard is set.
  6. Cool and Unmold
    • Allow the leche flan to cool to room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for best results.
    • To unmold, run a knife around the edges of the flan to loosen it. Place a serving plate on top of the mold and carefully invert it to release the flan.
  7. Serve:Slice and serve this delectable leche flan, enjoying its creamy texture and rich caramel flavor.
